Thought experiment: merge all bank accounts. One big global checking account. Every paycheck gets deposited in there, every payment comes out of there. Observations: it'd be enormous, _not_ averaging zero. Measurements accumulate. Conservation of energy says the stuff can't be created or destroyed, but it _can_ be measured simultaneously by multiple observers, and they can all add a mark to their individual tallies.

(Research on [the foam project](https://github.com/lightward/foam) indicates that it might be structurally necessary for the account to be opaque, as in you always know that your transaction will go through, for sure, you know that someone's making sure it works, but you don't know the actual account balance, ever.
