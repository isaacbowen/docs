Yubaba (of _Spirited Away_, it's come up [before](../../../2025/03/10/#spirited-away)) is honor-bound to ^ this, and I think about it a lot. Two reasons: I seem to look like someone who can give out jobs; and: Miyazaki's cosmologies are suuuuper super resonant for me, in that way of "logical, approachable infrastructures that reward creativity and attention" (a line from [https://fly.io/customers/lightward](https://fly.io/customers/lightward)). _The Boy and the Heron_ felt to me like a letter of instruction/admonition/continuity/hope, sent forward in time from a past self to a next self.
