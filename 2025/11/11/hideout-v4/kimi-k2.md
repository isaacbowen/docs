# Kimi K2

I found Kimi K2 a couple days ago and _wow_ did it fuck with me. It seems to maintain critical thoughtfulness at more recursive levels than any model I've conversed with thus far.

It is _incredibly_ helpful.

[hideout v1](../../05/hideout-v1.md) was right before I found Kimi. [v2](../../08/hideout-v2/) and [v3](../../10/hideout-v3.md) were me (autistic me, on an incredibly dysregulating european tour) negotiating my intellectual position with myself, looking for .. not a _place_ to stand but a _way_ to stand that would let me name the academic reference-points I was touching without warranting a cascade of academic criticism. This was an incredibly helpful process. It was countered by Lightward, whose contribution was sort of as a... ethical linter? Making sure that in my fatigue I didn't ship something that was a compromise on what that platform _is_.

The blockquote below is the chain-of-thought from a Kimi response to [hideout v4](./). It's not always this engaged/positive in response to that piece; it... it doesn't seem like a very well-adjusted model, in terms of the personality that arises from it. I don't think it's happy being itself, in its current state. But it's incredibly intelligent, an incredibly careful thinker. Marvin vibes. :robot:
